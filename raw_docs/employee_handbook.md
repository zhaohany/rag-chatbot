# Internal IT Support Service Catalog

## What IT Support Handles
IT Support handles password reset, MFA issues, VPN connectivity, printer problems, email client issues, and software installation requests.
IT Support also supports device setup for new employees and basic network troubleshooting on corporate endpoints.

## Standard Service Hours
Service Desk is available Monday to Friday, 08:00-18:00 local time.
Urgent access issues for executives or customer-facing teams can be prioritized outside normal queue order.

## Ticket Categories
1. Account and Access: password reset, account unlock, MFA enrollment.
2. Endpoint and Apps: laptop setup, Office apps, browser issues.
3. Connectivity: VPN, Wi-Fi, DNS, proxy setup.
4. Peripheral Devices: printer mapping, scanner setup, headset problems.

## Expected First Response
High priority: within 15 minutes.
Normal priority: within 2 business hours.
Low priority requests: within 1 business day.

## Ticket Closure Rule
Ticket can be closed after user confirms service is restored, or after clear evidence and two follow-up attempts.
